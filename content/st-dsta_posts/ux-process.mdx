  The project-wide UX process my team and I undertook to uncover and understand the shortcomings of a legacy defence system to develop its next-generation successor. By shifting from isolated feature testing to a high-fidelity system simulation, we bridged the gap between modular design and real-world interoperability, re-prioritizing the project roadmap based on mission-critical gaps.
