        col2: >
          How long is too long ago? Was it important to include the perspectives
          of someone who adopted a long time ago? On hindsight, yes, since the
          research goals were to discover the needs and pain points of the pet
          adoption process. However, in other scenarios with product-centric
          questions, it may not be useful as the product may have changed
          significantly since.
